2. Vitamin D (Cholecalciferol, Ergocalciferol)

¢ Properties: Fat-soluble, heat-stable, synthesized in skin upon sunlight exposure.
e Functions:

o Regulates calcium and phosphorus metabolism.

o Essential for bone and teeth formation.

o Helps prevent rickets (children) and osteomalacia (adults).

3. Vitamin E (Tocopherols, Tocotrienols)

¢ Properties: Fat-soluble, stable to heat, destroyed by oxidation.

¢ Functions:
o Acts as an antioxidant, protecting cells from oxidative damage.
o Supports immune function and skin health.

o Plays a role in red blood cell formation.

4. Vitamin K (Phylloquinone, Menaquinone)

¢ Properties: Fat-soluble, heat-stable, sensitive to light.
e Functions:
o Essential for blood clotting.

o Helps in bone metabolism.

Water-Soluble Vitamins

5. Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid)

¢ Properties: Water-soluble, sensitive to heat, light, and oxygen.
e Functions:
o Acts as an antioxidant.
o Essential for collagen synthesis (important for skin, cartilage, and wound healing).

o Enhances iron absorption.
